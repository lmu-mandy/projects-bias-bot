<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">Despite their long-standing differences during the Cold War, in 1975 the US and USSR embarked on a joint mission into space.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">The aim of the operation was to dock an American Apollo space capsule with a Soyuz one made by the Soviet Union.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">The collaboration took place at a time when it looked like diplomatic relations between the two sides were starting to improve.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">Millions of people watched a live feed of the rendezvous as it happened 200km above the earth.</p>
