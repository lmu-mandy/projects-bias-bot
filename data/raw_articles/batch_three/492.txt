<div><h1 class="headline">NYC gang of dirt bikers brutally beat 64-year-old man, son in caught-on-video attack</h1><p>A group of New York City dirt bikers rip a father and son from their car, then beat and rob them.</p>
<p class="speakable">Disturbing footage released by police shows a gang of dirt bikers viciously beat a father and son in broad daylight on a New York City street.</p>
<p class="speakable"></p>
<p>The 64-year-old motorist, accompanied by his 36-year-old son, collided with one of the dirt bikes on March 15 at about 4:30 p.m. in Upper Manhattan, according to police.</p>
<p>
      Gang of dirt bikers allegedly drag father, son from car in vicious NYC beat down.
      </p>
<p>The enraged mob of bikers allegedly pursued the motorist for two blocks, cutting him off with their vehicles near the intersection of Saint Nicholas Terrace and West 127th Street, the New York Police Department said. </p>
<p>
      Gang of dirt bikers assault 64-year-old motorist, son in Manhattan.
      </p>
<p>At least four bikers and one ATV rider participated in the beat down – allegedly dragging the father and son from their car, punching and kicking them in the middle of the street then robbing them of a cellphone, credit cards and cash, according to police.</p>
<p>
      Gang of NYC dirt bikers allegedly drag father, son from car and viciously beat them.
      </p>
<p>The victims suffered minor injuries and were treated at a nearby hospital. Police are asking for the public's help in identifying the suspects. </p>
<p></p>
<p>Anyone with information is urged to call NYPD's Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS. </p>
