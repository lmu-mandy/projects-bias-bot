<div><h1 class="headline">Tesla driver careens onto Texas sidewalk, nearly hits women, video shows</h1><p>A Tesla driver fell asleep behind the wheel on Wednesday, driving onto a sidewalk in Hamlin, Texas, and striking the city hall building. CREDIT: Hamlin Police Department via Storyful</p>
<p class="speakable">A driver who fell asleep behind the wheel of a Tesla careened onto a sidewalk in Hamlin, Texas, last week, just missing two women who had been taking a smoke break outside moments earlier, authorities said.</p>
<p class="speakable">Two female police dispatchers were smoking cigarettes while standing on the sidewalk outside the police department on Wednesday, Hamlin Police Chief Bobby Evans said.</p>
<p></p>
<p>CCTV footage shows that moments after the women head back inside, a Tesla speeds onto the sidewalk and crashes alongside the building.</p>
<p>
      The Tesla driver had fallen asleep at the wheel moments before careening onto a sidewalk in Hamlin, Texas, where two women had been standing moments earlier, authorities said.
      </p>
<p>Evans told KTXS-TV that the driver had fallen asleep as he was coming into Hamlin.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The driver, who was not immediately identified, hit the corner of the city hall building and knocked down the awning pillars of five store fronts, Evans said.</p>
<p>No further details were immediately provided.</p>
