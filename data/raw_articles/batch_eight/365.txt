<div><h1 class="ssrcss-15xko80-StyledHeading e1fj1fc10" id="main-heading" tabindex="-1">US Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi tests positive for Covid</h1><p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00"><b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">US Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi has tested positive for Covid-19, making her the most senior member of Congress so far to report an infection.</b></p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">The leading Democrat is asymptomatic, her spokesman said on Thursday. </p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">Mrs Pelosi, 82, was seen hugging former President Barack Obama during his visit to the White House on Tuesday and standing close to President Joe Biden.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">According to the latest US guidelines, quarantine is not required after Covid-19 exposure for those vaccinated.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">Both Mr Biden and Mr Obama have been vaccinated, and the current president tested negative on Wednesday, the White House said. He is not considered a close contact under the guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">Mrs Pelosi's spokesman Drew Hammill said: "The Speaker is fully vaccinated and boosted, and is thankful for the robust protection the vaccine has provided".</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">"The Speaker will quarantine consistent with CDC guidance, and encourages everyone to get vaccinated, boosted and test regularly".</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">The news of Mrs Pelosi's diagnosis comes as the US approaches 1 million Covid-19 related deaths since the start of the pandemic.</p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">After a steep spike in infections in December - fuelled by the highly contagious Omicron variant - Covid-19 cases and hospital admissions have fallen precipitously since mid-January. </p>
<p class="ssrcss-1q0x1qg-Paragraph eq5iqo00">But breakthrough infections have hit a number of high-powered Washington residents in recent days, with vaccinated leaders including Attorney General Merrick Garland, Commerce Secretary Gina Raimondo and US Congressman Adam Schiff announcing they had tested positive.</p>
