<div><h1 class="headline">6 injured in Chicago area shooting during gathering, police say</h1><p>Fox News Flash top headlines are here. Check out what's clicking on Foxnews.com.</p>
<p class="speakable">Six people were injured during a shooting in a west Chicago suburb early Sunday morning, police said. </p>
<p class="speakable">Officers responded to a residential neighborhood in Elgin just before 2 a.m. to find five victims with injuries, who were all taken to area hospitals. Officers later learned a sixth victim with injuries had taken themselves to a hospital. </p>
<p><br><br>Preliminary information is that the shooting happened during a gathering, and appears to be an isolated incident.<br><br>Detectives from the Major Investigations Division and Special Investigations Division are actively investigating the case and updates will be provided as they become available. <br><br></p>
<p><br><br>Anyone with information about the shooting is asked to call 847-289-2600, or text 847411 and include "ELGINPD" in the beginning of the text, along with the message and/or tip information. People can also visit this website for more crime tip options.</p>
<p>Prior to this shooting, the city of Elgin's gunfire dashboard shows 16 year-to-date incidents involving three victims. Last year, the city had a total of 66 gunshot victims.</p>
