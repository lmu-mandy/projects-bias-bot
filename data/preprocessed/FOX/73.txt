<div><h1 class="headline">New Jersey's American Dream mall shooting sends shoppers running for cover in nightmare scenario</h1><p>Fox News Flash top headlines are here. Check out what's clicking on Foxnews.com.</p>
<p class="speakable">Shots were fired early Thursday evening at the American Dream Mall in East Rutherford, New Jersey, sending shoppers fleeing into stores for cover. </p>
<p class="speakable">New Jersey Gov. Phil Murphy said he's been briefed by New Jersey State Police on the shooting and urged the public to stay away from the mall. </p>
<p>Videos shared on social media showed shoppers looking for cover inside the mall and a large police presence outside. </p>
<p>New Jersey State Police said the shooting happened at 5:50 p.m.</p>
<p></p>
<p>It wasn't immediately clear if anyone was struck by gunfire or if police have arrested any suspects. </p>
<p><span>Shots were fired at American Dream Mall in East Rutherford, New Jersey on Thursday. </span> <span>(Reuters/Eduardo Munoz)</span></p>
<p> <span>(Reuters/Eduardo Munoz)</span></p>
<p>Anna Papoutsakis was attending a realtor event at the mall when she was herded with others into a nearby store. </p>
<p>"About 10 minutes into the event they moved us into [Saks Fifth Avenue] and closed the gates," Papoutsakis told Fox News Digital. "Now police are checking the area to clear us." </p>
<p></p>
<p>A reunification site was opened at lot 26 for families, according to the New Jersey State Police. </p>
