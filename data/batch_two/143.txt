<div><h1 class="headline">Florida Highway Patrol trooper attacked on interstate and bystanders come to his aid, dashcam video shows</h1><p>Dashcam footage shows a suspect attacking a Florida Highway Patrol trooper before several bystanders pull over to help subdue the man on the interstate. </p>
<p class="speakable">The Florida Highway Patrol is sending its thanks to a group of good Samaritans who came to a trooper's aid after he was attacked on an interstate in Tampa on Friday. </p>
<p class="speakable">Dashcam video of the encounter shows the trooper informing the suspect, 24-year-old Alexander Hernandez Delgado, that he can't walk on the interstate. </p>
<p>
      Alexander Hernandez Delgado, 24, was arrested and charged with battery on a law enforcement officer and resisting an officer with violence. 
      </p>
<p>Delgado told the trooper that he's an artist and was walking on the roadway because his mom kicked him out of the house, the trooper wrote in an arrest report. </p>
<p></p>
<p>"I then searched the subject and attempted to place him in the back of my patrol car. The subject became argumentative and stated that I am bothering him and that he would like to be left alone," the trooper wrote in the report. "The subject then pulled away and then threw a punch with his left hand that [struck] me in the face."</p>
<p>
      Dashcam video shows the suspect punching the trooper in the face before the altercation continues off-camera. 
      </p>
<p>The video shows the suspect hitting the trooper in the face before the two go off-camera in the median, where the trooper wrote that Delgado struck him several more times. </p>
<p>After the trooper tackled Delgado, an 18-wheeler and another car pulled over and several bystanders jumped in to help the officer subdue Delgado. </p>
<p>"The FHP thanks all the Good Samaritans that stopped on I-4, in both directions, on Friday morning, March 18th in Tampa to help our Trooper," a spokesman for the FHP said in a statement. </p>
<p>
      Several bystanders pulled over and helped the trooper place Delgado under arrest. 
      </p>
<p></p>
<p>The trooper sustained a bloody nose as a result of the incident. </p>
<p>Delgado was transported to the Hillsborough County Jail and charged with battery on a law enforcement officer and resisting an officer with violence. </p>
