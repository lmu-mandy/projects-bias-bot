<p class="speakable">Two Pennsylvania State Troopers and a civilian are dead following an automobile accident in Philadelphia, authorities said.</p>
<p class="speakable">The incident occurred just before 1 a.m. Monday morning along I-95, Fox 29 reported.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Authorities said the three victims were all deceased at the scene. Authorities have not released any details surrounding the crash, Fox 29 reported.</p>

<p>Pennsylvania State Police are continuing to investigate the incident and traffic running both ways along I-95 has been shut down, according to the report.</p>
